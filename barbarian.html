Barbarian

Fueled by primal rage, the Barbarian charges into battle with unbridled ferocity. Whether wielding a massive greataxe or relying on raw strength alone, they are masters of physical combat. Their rage empowers them, granting increased resilience and unmatched combat prowess. Barbarians are often fearless warriors who thrive in the heat of battle, drawing strength from their primal instincts and unyielding determination.
