Bard

With a song on their lips and a melody in their heart, the Bard weaves magic through music and storytelling. Versatile and charismatic, they inspire allies with stirring performances and beguile foes with enchanting spells. Whether bolstering allies with words of encouragement or disrupting enemies with dissonant chords, Bards are the ultimate performers, blending magic with artistry to shape the course of destiny.
