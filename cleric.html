Cleric

Devoted to their chosen deity or cause, the Cleric channels divine power to heal the wounded, protect the innocent, and smite the wicked. They are the conduits of divine will, wielding holy symbols and prayers to invoke miracles and banish darkness. Whether serving as a beacon of hope in times of despair or leading the charge against the forces of evil, Clerics are steadfast guardians of faith and righteousness.
