Druid

Attuned to the natural world, the Druid is a master of wild magic and guardian of the balance between civilization and wilderness. They draw power from the primal forces of nature, shapeshifting into beasts, calling upon the elements, and communing with spirits of the land. With a deep connection to the earth and its creatures, Druids embody the harmony of nature, wielding their power to protect the wild places of the world.
