Fighter

Disciplined and skilled in combat, the Fighter is a master of arms, honing their prowess through rigorous training and battlefield experience. Whether wielding a sword and shield, a bow, or a pair of deadly daggers, Fighters excel in all forms of combat, adapting to any situation with unmatched versatility. They are the stalwart guardians of civilization, standing as bulwarks against the forces of chaos and tyranny.
