Paladin

Bound by oath and imbued with divine power, the Paladin is a holy warrior dedicated to upholding justice and righteousness. Clad in shining armor and wielding a weapon blessed by their deity, they smite evil with righteous fury and protect the innocent with unwavering resolve. Paladins are beacons of hope in a dark world, embodying the virtues of courage, compassion, and sacrifice.
