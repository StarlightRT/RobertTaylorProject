Ranger

Masters of the wilderness, Rangers are skilled hunters and trackers who roam the untamed wilds in search of their quarry. Whether wielding a bow, a pair of finely crafted knives, or forging a bond with a loyal animal companion, Rangers excel at surviving in the harshest environments and striking swiftly and decisively against their foes. They are the guardians of the natural world, defending it from the encroachment of civilization and the forces of darkness.
