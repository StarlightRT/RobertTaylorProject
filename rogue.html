Rogue

Cunning and agile, the Rogue is a master of stealth and deception, adept at slipping into the shadows and striking from the darkness. Whether picking locks, disarming traps, or delivering deadly sneak attacks, Rogues excel at navigating the treacherous paths of intrigue and subterfuge. They are the ultimate opportunists, using their wit and guile to outmaneuver foes and emerge victorious against all odds.
