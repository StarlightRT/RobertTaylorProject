Sorcerer

Born with innate magical talent, the Sorcerer wields raw arcane power with instinctual ease. Their magic flows from their very bloodline, granting them abilities that defy comprehension. Whether conjuring flames with a flick of their fingers, bending reality to their will, or summoning otherworldly entities, Sorcerers are conduits of primal magic, shaping the world with their thoughts and emotions.
