Spells

  • Acid Splash - You hurl a bubble of acid.
  • Burning Hands - A cone of searing flame.
  • Charm Person - You attempt to charm a humanoid.
  • Cure Wounds - A creature you touch regains a number of hit points equal to 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier.
  • Detect Magic - For the duration, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet of you.
  • Disguise Self - You make yourself, including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person, look different until the spell ends or until you use your action to dismiss it.
  • Entangle - Grasping weeds and vines sprout from the ground in a 20-foot square starting from a point within range.
  • Expeditious Retreat - This spell allows you to move at an incredible pace.
  • Faerie Fire - Each object in a 20-foot cube within range is outlined in blue, green, or violet light (your choice).
  • Feather Fall - Choose up to five falling creatures within range.
  • Find Familiar - You gain the service of a familiar, a spirit that takes an animal form you choose.
  • Fireball - A ball of fire erupts from your hands and hurtles toward a location you specify.
  • Grease - Slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square centered on a point within range and turns it into difficult terrain for the duration.
  • Guiding Bolt - A flash of light streaks toward a creature of your choice within range.
  • Hellish Rebuke - You point your finger, and the creature that damaged you is momentarily surrounded by hellish flames.
  • Healing Word - A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points.
  • Identify - You choose one object that you must touch throughout the casting of the spell.
  • Invisibility - A creature you touch becomes invisible until the spell ends.
  • Jump - You touch a creature and their jump height triples.
  • Longstrider - You touch a creature and they run like the wind adding 5 ft to their movement.
  • Mage Armor - You touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor.
  • Mage Hand - A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range.
  • Magic Missile - You create three glowing darts of magical force.
  • Message - You point your finger toward a creature within range and whisper a message.
  • Protection from Evil and Good - Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against certain types of creatures: aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead.
  • Ray of Frost - A frigid beam of blue-white light streaks toward a creature within range.
  • Ray of Sickness - A ray of sickening greenish energy lashes out toward a creature within range.
  • Sacred Flame - Flame-like radiance descends on a creature that you can see within range.
  • Sleep - This spell sends creatures into a magical slumber.
  • Thunderous Smite - The first time you hit with a melee weapon attack during this spell's duration, your weapon rings with thunder.
  • Thunderwave - A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you.
  • Witch Bolt - A beam of crackling blue energy lances out toward a creature within range.
