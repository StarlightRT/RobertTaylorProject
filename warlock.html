Warlock

Bound by pact and fueled by eldritch power, the Warlock serves a mysterious patron in exchange for otherworldly abilities. Whether striking bargains with ancient beings from beyond the stars or forging pacts with dark entities of the underworld, Warlocks wield dark magic with a price. They are agents of cosmic forces, wielding eldritch power to achieve their goals and unravel the secrets of the multiverse.
