Wizard

Scholars of the arcane arts, Wizards unlock the secrets of magic through diligent study and experimentation. With a spellbook in hand and a mind as sharp as a razor, they command the very fabric of reality itself. Whether hurling fireballs, conjuring protective wards, or unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos, Wizards are masters of spellcraft, shaping the forces of magic to suit their whims and desires.
